Member Home Redesign

The USAA app is the primary access point for members to interact with their accounts, policies, and services, with a focus on quick access to product information, servicing actions, and automatically prioritized content.

This is an effort to overhaul the design and performance of the USAA iOS, Android, and iPadOS apps. Starting from the groundwork laid in our website redesign, my team redesigned the IA and structure, while helping to develop a new design language and balance platform conventions with brand presence.
